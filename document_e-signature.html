Document And E-signature

The Document and E-Signature module enables secure handling of loan-related documents within the Loan Management System. Members can upload signed agreements, identity proofs, and other necessary files. Administrators can review, verify, and archive documents digitally. Integrated e-signature functionality allows for convenient, legally binding approvals, enhancing efficiency, reducing paperwork, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.
